he / she / it → has
I / you / we / they → have

❌ Verneinung

4⚖️ Present Perfect vs. Simple Past
Simple Past: abgeschlossene Handlung mit konkretem Zeitwort (yesterday, last week, in 2020).
Present Perfect: Bezug zur Gegenwart, kein konkretes Zeitwort (just, already, yet, ever, never, recently).
