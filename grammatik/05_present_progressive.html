Present Progressive

Was passiert gerade jetzt? · am/is/are + Verb-ing

⚡ Regeln auf einen Blick
1
✅ Aussagesatz
Subjekt+am/is/are+Verb-ing
I → am
you → are
he → is
she → is
it → is
we → are
you (Pl.) → are
they → are
I am playing football.
She is reading a book.
They are watching TV.
2
❌ Verneinung
Subjekt+am/is/are not+Verb-ing
Kurzformen: I'm not · isn't · aren't
nach am/is/are kommt einfach not.
I am not playing football.
He isn't reading.
They aren't watching TV.
3
❓ Ja/Nein-Frage
Am/Is/Are+Subjekt+Verb-ing?
Form von to be an den Anfang!
💬 Short Answer: Yes, I am. · No, he isn't.
Are they playing football? – Yes, they are.
Is she reading? – No, she isn't.
4
🔵 W-Frage
W-Wort+am/is/are+Subjekt+Verb-ing?
What · Where · Why · Who · How
– immer ganz vorne!
What are you doing?
Where is she going?
5
⏱ Wann benutze ich es?
jetzt gerade ·im Moment
Das Present Progressive beschreibt:
• was gerade jetzt passiert
• etwas, das im Moment abläuft
⚡ Simple Present = immer/oft · Present Progressive = jetzt!
Look! He is running. (jetzt gerade)
I am writing right now. (im Moment)
They are playing outside. (jetzt)
6
📌 Signalwörter
now right now at the moment at the minute Look! Listen!
Diese Wörter zeigen: etwas passiert jetzt gerade.
⚠️ Look! und Listen! stehen meist am Satzanfang.
She is cooking now.
We are working at the moment.
Look! He is running.
